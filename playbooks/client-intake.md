# Playbook: Client Intake

Use before accepting external work.

## Intake Questions

## Business

- What outcome do you want?
- Who will use it?
- What does success look like?
- What is the deadline?
- What is the budget range?

## Product

- What workflow exists today?
- What is painful about it?
- What must be included?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- Who approves the result?

## Technical

- Existing codebase or new build?
- Required stack?
- Required integrations?
- Hosting environment?
- Auth, payments, data, compliance?

## Delivery

- Milestones?
- Revision policy?
- Handoff expectation?
- Maintenance expectation?

## Output

```markdown
# Client Brief

## Goal
## Users
## Scope
## Non-Goals
## Milestones
## Acceptance Criteria
## Risks
## Questions Before Quote
```
